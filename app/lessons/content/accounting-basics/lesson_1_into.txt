Accounting is the systematic process of recording, summarizing, and reporting financial transactions. It provides essential information for decision-making and financial management in businesses. Beginners learn about the accounting equation, which forms the foundation of double-entry bookkeeping: Assets = Liabilities + Equity. Understanding accounts, ledgers, and journals is crucial for accurate record-keeping. In this lesson, you will explore the types of accounts, the principles of debit and credit, and how transactions affect the financial position of an organization. Accounting ensures transparency, compliance, and efficiency in managing financial resources.
