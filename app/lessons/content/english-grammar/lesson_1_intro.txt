English grammar is the set of rules that govern the structure of sentences in the English language. It includes parts of speech, sentence construction, punctuation, and syntax. Beginners focus on nouns, pronouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, prepositions, conjunctions, and interjections. Understanding subject-verb agreement, tenses, and sentence types is crucial for clear communication. This lesson introduces basic grammar rules, helping learners write and speak correctly while building a strong foundation for more advanced language skills.
